# Quick Guide: Change Your Netlify URL

## 3 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Go to Netlify
1. Open: https://app.netlify.com
2. Log in

### Step 2: Change Site Name
1. Click on your site (currently named something like `portfolio-website`)
2. Click **Site settings** (⚙️ gear icon at the top)
3. Scroll to **Site details** section
4. Find **Site name** 
5. Click **Change site name**
6. Type your new name (e.g., `mahadharsan`)
7. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Your New URL
Your new URL will be: `https://mahadharsan.netlify.app` (or whatever name you chose)

**That's it!** The old URL will automatically redirect to the new one.
