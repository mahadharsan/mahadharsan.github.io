# How to Change Your Netlify Site Name (Free)

## Quick Steps

1. **Go to Netlify Dashboard**
   - Visit: https://app.netlify.com
   - Log in to your account

2. **Select Your Site**
   - Click on your site: `portfolio-website` (or whatever it's currently named)

3. **Change Site Name**
   - Click on **Site settings** (gear icon in the top menu)
   - Go to **General** â†’ **Site details**
   - Find **Site name** section
   - Click **Change site name** button
   - Enter your new name (e.g., `mahadharsan`)
   - Click **Save**

4. **Your New URL**
   - Your new URL will be: `https://[your-new-name].netlify.app`
   - Example: `https://mahadharsan.netlify.app`

## Important Notes

- **Site names must be unique** - If `mahadharsan` is taken, try:
  - `mahadharsan-portfolio`
  - `mahadharsan-data`
  - `mahadharsan-ravichandran`
  - `mahadharsan-dev`

- **URL changes immediately** - Your old URL will redirect to the new one automatically

- **After changing**, let me know your new URL and I'll update:
  - Open Graph meta tags
  - Any other references to the old URL

## What Happens to the Old URL?

- The old URL (`gleaming-sable-fce4b1.netlify.app`) will automatically redirect to your new URL
- This is permanent and free
- No broken links!
